# Contributing to SE-1 Framework

Thank you for your interest in the SE-1 (Subject + Object + Variable + Timeline = Output) Framework! Whether you are a researcher, developer, cognitive scientist, or simply someone looking to optimize decision-making, your contributions are highly welcome.

## 🤝 How to Collaborate

### 1. Open a Discussion
If you have questions, want to share how you applied SE-1 in a unique domain, or just want to brainstorm, please open a thread in the **Discussions** tab.

### 2. Submit a Case Study
We highly encourage real-world validations. To submit a new case study:
- Fork the repository.
- Create a new markdown file in `docs/case-studies/`.
- Follow the structure: **Context (S+O)** -> **Variables (V)** -> **Timeline (T)** -> **Probabilistic Output** -> **Real-world Validation**.
- Submit a Pull Request (PR).

### 3. Report Issues / Suggest Improvements
Found a flaw in the logic or want to suggest an optimization to the S+O+V+T mechanism? Open an **Issue** using the `Framework Enhancement` template.

## 🤖 The AI & Metaphor Synergy (Crucial for AI Integration)

One of the core strengths of SE-1 is its ability to process **non-linear patterns**. It connects seemingly unrelated domains (e.g., mapping military hardware to cognitive deficits in geopolitics).

**Note for AI Researchers and Prompt Engineers:**
Because SE-1 operates on non-linear cognitive mapping, **the effectiveness of an AI in processing, discussing, and generating SE-1 outputs is directly proportional to the richness of its metaphorical dataset.**

- **Linear AI** (trained mostly on rigid, technical, or procedural data) will struggle to map the cross-domain variables of SE-1.
- **Metaphor-Rich AI** (trained on literature, poetry, cross-disciplinary analogies, and abstract philosophy) possesses a latent space capable of connecting distant conceptual nodes. This makes it significantly more effective at translating non-linear human uncertainty into the structured S+O+V+T format.

*When using SE-1 with LLMs, ensure your system prompts encourage metaphorical reasoning and cross-domain analogical thinking to maximize the framework's precision.*
